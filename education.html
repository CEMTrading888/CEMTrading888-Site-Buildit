// INDICATORS EXPLAINED

INTERMEDIATE

EMA Crossover Strategy

The 9/21 EMA cross is one of the cleanest trend-following signals. When the fast EMA crosses above the slow EMA, momentum is shifting bullish. Add MACD for confirmation.

INTERMEDIATE

VWAP as a Filter

Volume Weighted Average Price tells you where institutional money is positioned. Trading above VWAP = bullish bias. Below = bearish. Use it to filter your entries.

INTERMEDIATE

ATR for Stop Losses

Average True Range measures volatility. Setting stops at 1.5x ATR means your stop adapts to market conditions — wider in volatile sessions, tighter in quiet ones.

// RISK MANAGEMENT

ESSENTIAL

The $150 Daily Loss Rule

Never lose more than $150 in a single day. Once you hit that limit, close the platform. This single rule prevents the catastrophic losing days that wipe out weeks of gains.

ESSENTIAL

Position Sizing

1 micro contract per trade. Never add to a losing position. Scale up only after 30+ consecutive profitable days. Slow and steady wins in algo trading.

ESSENTIAL

Paper Trading First

Run every new strategy in paper trading (simulation) for at least 2 weeks before going live. If it can't perform in simulation, it won't perform with real money.
